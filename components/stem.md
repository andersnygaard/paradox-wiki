# Stem

The Paradox's bow stem: the timber it is made from, its notching, and how it is assembled into the hull.

The stem post is 100 mm (4") thick. A sculling oar has no reverse and no brake, so contact with an obstruction while sculling is unavoidable — the stem's thickness is what leaves the boat undamaged when it happens.[1]
