# Dave's Wiki (aiki.pbworks.com)

**Dave's Wiki** is a public PBworks wiki (`aiki.pbworks.com`, primarily an aikido/personal notebook) compiling technical detail on Matt Layden's **[Enigma](/matt-layden/enigma.md)**. It was pointed to from [Dave Bolduc's microcruising.com](/references/microcruising-website.md) ("Dave's Wiki … 'Advantages of Matt Layden mini cruisers'"). Two of its pages are directly relevant to this bundle:

- **"Advantages of Matt Layden mini cruisers"** (`/w/page/1594232/…`) — a side-by-side treatment of the [Paradox](/paradox.md) and the **Enigma 460**, organized by shared feature (shallow draft, chine runners, enclosed cabin/cockpit, standing headroom, removable hatches, safety, watertight compartments, rig, roller furling, sweep-oar propulsion, history), with many photographs of both boats.

- **"Enigma sailboat"** (`/w/page/1594388/…`) — a detailed, working builder's notebook on the Enigma family (**360** and **460**), with full design particulars, a Paradox/360/460 comparison table, construction schedules, a ballast-materials analysis, and quotations from Matt Layden and others.

## Nature and reliability of the source

This is a **secondary/compiling source**, distinct from and generally lower in authority than microcruising.com: compiled by an enthusiast/builder, not Matt Layden or a boat's owner, and much of the value is in the **primary quotations collected** (notably from Matt Layden himself, c. 2006, and from the builder of the Enigma 460 prototype). Its own measurements and estimates are flagged as such ("est.") and treated with corresponding caution. Where it overlaps microcruising.com (e.g. the Enigma "sixth micro-cruiser" summary), it largely quotes that source verbatim.

## Provenance and dating

The wiki is a live PBworks site but its relevant pages are effectively static: the "Advantages" page was last edited **~September 2010** and the "Enigma sailboat" page **~November 2012** (PBworks "N years ago" stamps as read on 2026-07-02); content spans **2005–2012**, centered on the Enigma 460 prototype's 2010 sea trials. Both pages, plus **all 30 wiki-hosted images**, were retrieved on 2026-07-02 and mirrored locally under `source-web/daves-wiki/` (see `source-web/daves-wiki/MANIFEST.tsv`), so citations stay verifiable.
