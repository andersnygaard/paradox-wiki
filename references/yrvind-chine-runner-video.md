transcript imported to this bundle under `source-youtube/`.

Like [yrvind.com](/references/yrvind-website.md) itself, this is an **authored,

twenty-eight years after the events it recounts. It is firsthand authority on the

subscription, the Jensen Beach / Stuart stay, and figures for Yrvind's own
chine-runner Atlantic boat — it is recorded as a new data point, cited to the
transcript.
