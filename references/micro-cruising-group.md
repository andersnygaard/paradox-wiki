# Micro Cruising (Yahoo group)

**"Micro Cruising"** is a Yahoo group founded and moderated by
[Dave Bolduc](/people/bolduc/dave.md), started around September 2001 for
broader micro-cruising discussion beyond the single Paradox design. It is a
sibling community to the
[Paradox builders mailing list](/references/mailing-list.md), and its link
was circulated on `paradoxbuilders` as a place for Paradox members to join.

- Original URL (2001): `http://groups.yahoo.com/group/microcruising`

Micro Cruising is a standard pointer for newcomers asking where to learn more
about Paradox or where to buy plans — worth checking both its photos and its
file section — alongside Dave Bolduc's standing as the sole authorized seller
of the plans (see [building plans](/references/building-plans.md)). Nearly a
year after founding the group, Dave Bolduc remained its moderator, running
"the microcruising forum" (see
[first-boat suitability discussion](/operations/first-boat-suitability.md)).

Topics raised on `paradoxbuilders` were sometimes cross-posted to Micro
Cruising as well, e.g. [Glen C. Maxwell](/people/glen-maxwell.md)'s rebuttal
on plywood grades and foam flotation safety — see
[plywood grade and foam flotation debate](/construction/plywood-grades-and-foam-flotation.md).
