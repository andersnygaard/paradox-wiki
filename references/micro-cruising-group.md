# Micro Cruising (Yahoo group)

**"Micro Cruising"** is a Yahoo group started around September 2001 for broader micro-cruising discussion beyond the single Paradox design. It is a sibling community to the [Paradox builders mailing list](/references/mailing-list.md), and its link was circulated on `paradoxbuilders` as a place for Paradox members to join.

- Original URL (2001): `http://groups.yahoo.com/group/microcruising`

Micro Cruising is a standard pointer for newcomers asking where to learn more about Paradox or where to buy plans — worth checking both its photos and its file section — alongside the sole authorized seller of the plans (see [building plans](/references/building-plans.md)). Nearly a year after founding, the group's moderator was still running "the microcruising forum" (see [first-boat suitability discussion](/operations/first-boat-suitability.md)).

Topics raised on `paradoxbuilders` were sometimes cross-posted to Micro Cruising as well, e.g. a rebuttal on plywood grades and foam flotation safety — see [plywood grade and foam flotation debate](/construction/plywood-grades-and-foam-flotation.md).

## A recurring request for the group's own link

Even years into the archive, established members occasionally asked the list itself for Micro Cruising's link, rather than finding it via `microcruising.com` — a January 2006 instance was answered twice over within about an hour, once with the direct Yahoo Groups URL and once with the `microcruising.com` homepage, underscoring how often the two pointers (the Yahoo group and the same family's own site) circulate as interchangeable answers to the same question.

## Citing this source

**Micro Cruising** was a Yahoo Group at `groups.yahoo.com/group/microcruising`. Yahoo shut down every Yahoo Group, archives included, in December 2020, so that URL no longer resolves and the group's own message archive no longer exists anywhere public. The stable identifier is the **group name**, together with a message reference where one is known. Cite in the form:

> [Micro Cruising (Yahoo group)](/references/micro-cruising-group.md) — [detail].

None of the citations below carry an individual Micro Cruising message number; they are dated mentions of the group from the [Paradox builders mailing list](/references/mailing-list.md) instead.
