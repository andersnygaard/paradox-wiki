# microcruising.com (David & Mindy Bolduc's website)

**microcruising.com** (`http://www.microcruising.com/`) is a personal "Micro Cruising" website tied to the distributor of the [Paradox building plans](/references/building-plans.md), the ownership and cruising of the Layden-designed [Little Cruiser](/matt-layden/little-cruiser.md), the restoration of [Swamp Thing](/matt-layden/swamp-thing.md), and the later purchase of Matt Layden's [Enigma](/matt-layden/enigma.md). It is distinct from the [Micro Cruising **Yahoo group**](/references/micro-cruising-group.md); this concept covers the **website** itself.

Unlike the [mailing-list archive](/references/mailing-list.md) (many builders' back-and-forth email), this is an **authored, first-person primary source** — an unusually authoritative one, given personal acquaintance with Matt Layden, firsthand examination of most of his boats, and three decades of firsthand micro-cruising experience behind it. Where the two sources overlap they largely corroborate each other; where the website adds detail (exact design particulars, the full boat lineage with dates, the designer's stated design rationale, and long how-to articles), it is treated as high-quality primary evidence, while clearly-labeled opinions ("in our opinion…") are recorded as opinion rather than fact.

## Provenance and dating

The site is **effectively frozen**: its files were last modified around **December 2019** (the most recent content is a 2019 Cedar Key gathering notice and blog entries running to ~2018), and its newest dated article material runs through the early-to-mid 2010s. It was **retrieved for this bundle on 2026-07-02** and mirrored locally under `source-web/microcruising/` — the full snapshot: **135 HTML pages plus all 1,643 referenced images** (thumbnails, inline photos, design-particular diagrams, and the full-size gallery photos linked from it; ~57 MB total) — for reproducible citation. Because it is a static historical snapshot, its content is cited in the past tense and dated where the page gives a date, consistent with the bundle's historical-archive convention.

## Site structure (what lives where)

- **Boats** — Matt Layden's designs, each with a design-particulars box and history: "Matt's Boats" overview (`pictures.htm`), `paradox1.htm`, `swampthing1.htm`, `lc1.htm`, `other1.htm` (Terrapin & GJAC), `Enigma.htm`, `Elusion.htm`, plus study plans (`plans1.htm`), Don Elliott's sketches (`Sketch1.htm`), and tank-test models (`models.htm`).
- **Articles** (`Articlemain.htm`) — long-form pieces: the Small Craft Advisor interview (`sca1.htm`), "Cruising in Little Cruiser" (`article1.htm`), "Cheating Winter" (`article2.htm`), the "Microcruising Guide" (`mcg.htm`), "Secrets to Cruising" (`secrets.htm`), "Sculling With a Yuloh" (`WLL.htm`), a Cruising World piece (`cw1.htm`), book reviews (`reviewb.htm`, `sailing.htm`), and forum-question commentaries (`fc.htm`, `fq1.htm`, `fq2.htm`).
- **Projects** (`mainprojects.htm`) — how-to build/refit logs: hull repair, Little Cruiser refit, Swamp Thing rebuild, folding dinghy, cushions, painting, bow shed, sailmaking, and LED lighting.
- **Trips / Blog / News** — cruising reports and the news/blog archives (`list.htm`, `new1a.htm`, `new2a.htm`, `oldnews.htm`, trip pages, etc.), including the annual **Cedar Key** small-boat gathering.
- **Reviews / Links / misc** — recommended books and gear, "Famous Boats" (`famoussmallboats.htm`), external links, and a provisioning food list.
