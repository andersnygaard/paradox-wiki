# Wind Vane Self-Steering (book)

***Wind-Vane Self-Steering*** (attributed on the list to "Bill Belcher") is a
recommended reference for a "very simple, very cheap" **plywood wind vane**
judged well suited to the [Paradox](/paradox.md), offered as a candidate
answer to the community's open [self-steering](/operations/self-steering.md)
question.

## Availability (December 2001): out of print, but purchasable

As of December 2001 the book was **out of print** — a check of **Amazon.com**
and **BarnesAndNoble.com** turned up no copies. It remained available,
however, from **armchairsailor.com** for **$36.95**.

A second, independently recommended reference on the same subject is John
Letcher's ***Self-Steering for Sailing Craft***, pointed to by [William
Longyard](/people/william-longyard.md) as the best available guide to
designing a working vane/paddle self-steering gear for a boat as small as
Paradox — see [self-steering — auto-pilot versus wind-vane self-steering: a
practical
comparison](/operations/self-steering.md#auto-pilot-versus-wind-vane-self-steering-a-practical-comparison).
