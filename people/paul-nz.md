# Paul (NZ)

**Paul** ("Paul - NZ") is a prospective builder in the **south of New Zealand**
who joined the `paradoxbuilders` list in July 2001. He was drawn to the Paradox
for two reasons that speak to the boat's appeal: the ability to sail from within
the [enclosed cabin](/paradox.md) in the region's often-rough weather, and the
boat's simple, robust rig — a contrast to earlier lake trips of his that had been
spoiled by fancy equipment failing in strong winds.

## Questions on plans, weight, and construction

His introduction laid out the practical concerns of a new builder: where to get
the plans and their cost (from Dave Bolduc for **$35 US**, see
[building plans](/references/building-plans.md)); the empty trailered weight and
day-sailing suitability; handling in a 2–4 ft fresh-water chop; where to obtain
[Don Elliott](/people/don-elliott.md)'s second building article, having already
downloaded the first; and the [plywood count and thicknesses](/design/hull-scantlings.md).
