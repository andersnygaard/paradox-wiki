# Mark Waters

**Mark Waters** (Yahoo profile `mwatersworld`) is a Los Angeles, California Paradox builder who introduced himself to the list in February 2006 while registering his boat with a UK build registry. At that point his Paradox — built strictly to the plans, with no name yet chosen — was **90% complete**, with completion expected by summer 2006. He had also built a Selway-Fisher 50/50 sailing canoe before turning to the Paradox.

Waters asked to correspond with any other Paradox builders or owners in the Los Angeles area; a query followed asking him to clarify whether "L.A." meant the city proper or the wider county, hoping to see the boat on a future visit. Waters had no objection to having his e-mail address listed alongside his registry entry when the idea was raised shortly afterward. The e-mail address on file for Waters was subsequently flagged as apparently incorrect, with a request for him to get in touch to fix it.[5] Full account of the registry episode at [references/paradox-uk-website.md](/references/paradox-uk-website.md#a-2006-database-renewal-and-a-new-los-angeles-builder).

Photographs of Waters' finished Paradox were later called "superb," with plans to add them to the UK Paradox site. See [boats/faith.md](/boats/faith.md#chine-runner-and-gudgeon-photos-and-mark-waters-paradox).[6]

The gallery link was then posted to the group, and Waters' finished boat drew unprompted praise from three quarters: "incredibly beautiful" job; the interior finish singled out as looking especially well done from what could be seen in the photos; and the standard of finish admired along with a joke about lacking the patience for that quality of work.[7][8][9]

Waters was one of only two respondents to an August 2006 group poll on ballast weight, voting for **300 lb** — see [weight, ballast, and displacement — ballast](/design/weight-and-ballast.md#ballast).[11]
