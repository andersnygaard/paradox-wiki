# Sean Mulligan

**Sean Mulligan** owns the Paradox **[Scout](/boats/scout.md)** (originally built and named **"Tardis"** by Lezlie Henson), sailed out of **Lake Havasu, Arizona**. He founded the **Havasu Pocket Cruiser's Convention** and, with his wife Jo, also owns and sails a trailer sailer named **"Dauntless."** His acquisition of Tardis, her renaming, and her subsequent launch routine, heavy-wind sailing, and 2014 Texas 200 cruise are recorded in full on [Scout](/boats/scout.md).

## Online presence

Mulligan documents his sailing on an older Blogspot blog ("Full n By," `fullnby.blogspot.com`) and a newer site (`fullnby.com`), and on a YouTube channel (`youtube.com/user/nebwest2`) carrying Paradox sailing footage, photos, and trip accounts.[1][2]

## Disambiguation

Sean Mulligan is a different person from [Sean Herron](/people/sean-herron.md), an unrelated, already-documented Paradox correspondent; the two share only a first name.
