# Sean Mulligan

**Sean Mulligan** was the owner of the Paradox **[Scout](/boats/scout.md)** (originally built and named **"Tardis"** by [Lezlie Henson](/people/lezlie-henson.md)), sailed out of **Lake Havasu, Arizona**. He founded the **Havasu Pocket Cruiser's Convention** and, with his wife Jo, also owns and sails a trailer sailer named **"Dauntless."** His purchase of Tardis, his completion of her, her launch as "Scout" on 12 September 2013, and her subsequent launch routine, heavy-wind sailing, and 2014 Texas 200 cruise are recorded in full on [Scout](/boats/scout.md); he later sold her on to **[Chris Curtis](/boats/curtis-paradox.md)** of Sandpoint, Idaho.

## Background

Away from sailing, Mulligan is a **career firefighter** — he works shift "tours" on a department whose risk-management model he later mapped onto his sailing — and a **licensed commercial pilot** who also holds sailplane, aerobatic commercial-sailplane, and commercial hot-air-balloon ratings, having given aerobatic glider rides to paying passengers and flown corporate advertising balloons before he took up small-boat sailing more seriously.[3][4] The **Havasu Pocket Cruiser's Convention** he founded grew into the largest US trailer-sailer gathering of its day and a six-to-seven-month-a-year undertaking; he paused it after the 2013 event, hoping to revive it in 2015.[5][1] He sails with his wife **Jo**; their Golden Doodle **Ensign** crews aboard, taken on after the loss of an earlier dog, **Bosun**, for whom Mulligan's Lake Havasu night-sail event, the "Bosunator Challenge," is named.[5]

## Online presence

Mulligan documents his sailing on the Blogger blog **[Full n By](/references/full-n-by-blog.md)** (`fullnby.blogspot.com`) — mirrored and held for this archive as a first-hand primary source on finishing and sailing "Scout" — a newer self-hosted site (`fullnby.com`, which he moved to in January 2014), and a YouTube channel (`youtube.com/user/nebwest2`) carrying Paradox sailing footage, photos, and trip accounts.[1][2]

## Disambiguation

Sean Mulligan is a different person from [Sean Herron](/boats/defiant.md), an unrelated, already-documented Paradox correspondent; the two share only a first name.
