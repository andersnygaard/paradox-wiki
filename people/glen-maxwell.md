# Glen C. Maxwell

**Glen C. Maxwell** is a professional boat builder who founded the original `paradoxbuilders` Yahoo group on 7 July 2001 to exchange information on building and sailing the Paradox, and moderated it for over a year. He built the Paradox named **"Zoë"**, and previously worked at **Gold Coast Yachts** building large (~53 ft) wood-and-epoxy charter catamarans. Beyond his own build, Glen is a valuable firsthand witness of **Matt Layden** — the Paradox's designer — under sail: he was the one builder to sail his own Paradox alongside Matt's original at Ft. Desoto just before the 2003 Everglades Challenge, met him again at the 2003 Cedar Key Messabout, and was among the first to reply when Matt Layden posted to the list himself. (For the longest and closest firsthand relationship with the designer, see the [Bolducs](/people/bolduc.md), who knew Matt from 1992 and owned his boats.)

His record spans three distinguishable roles:

* [Build and "Zoë"](/people/glen-maxwell/build-and-zoe.md) — his own build and "Zoë" history: launch, sailing and trip reports, and the extensive construction, painting, rot, epoxy, and rigging Q&A he gave the list as a fellow builder.
* [List founder and moderator](/people/glen-maxwell/list-founder-and-moderator.md) — founding the group, the recurring "where do I get plans?" FAQ, welcoming newcomers, the tripod-link spam saga, and recruiting volunteer co-moderators (Dave Bolduc, Paul V., David LeBlanc).
* [Matt Layden eyewitness record](/people/glen-maxwell/matt-layden-eyewitness.md) — his firsthand, in-person Matt Layden material: the Ft. Desoto sail-alongside before the 2003 Everglades Challenge, the haul-out-roller sketch, the Cedar Key Messabout meeting where Matt's foot-pressure furling technique was revealed, and welcoming Matt Layden's own first-ever list post. This is primary-goal content for research on Matt Layden himself.
