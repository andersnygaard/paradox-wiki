# The Bolducs — David & Mindy Bolduc

**David and Mindy Bolduc** of Greensboro, North Carolina — Dave a carpenter,
Mindy a pharmacist — are the archive's most seasoned micro-cruising couple, with
roughly 30 years and a string of two-up winter Bahamas crossings behind them.
Dave distributes the [Paradox building plans](/references/building-plans.md) and
founded the companion [Micro Cruising](/references/micro-cruising-group.md) Yahoo
group; together they own and cruise [Little Cruiser](/matt-layden/little-cruiser.md),
a boat "very similar to Paradox," which anchors the couple's own site,
[microcruising.com](/references/microcruising-website.md).

Above all, the Bolducs are the archive's **deepest firsthand authorities on
[Matt Layden](/matt-layden/biography.md)** himself. Dave met Matt in 1992 and
bought Little Cruiser that year; the couple went on to own three Layden designs,
cruise the Bahamas in Matt's company for weeks at a time, and discuss each of his
boats with the designer at length — a relationship far longer and closer than any
single sail-alongside in the record. Their firsthand knowledge of the designer,
his boats, and his methods threads through the whole bundle.

The Bolduc record spans three pages:

* [Dave Bolduc](/people/bolduc/dave.md) — the carpenter and plans distributor:
  his boats (Little Cruiser, Swamp Thing, Enigma), his role on the list, and the
  wide body of design, construction, and cruising knowledge he contributed, much
  of it relayed directly from Matt Layden.
* [Mindy Bolduc](/people/bolduc/mindy.md) — pharmacist and co-skipper of Little
  Cruiser: the two-up Bahamas crossings, helming a severe overnight gale, and her
  own trip reports.
* [Matt Layden firsthand record](/people/bolduc/matt-layden-authority.md) — the
  couple's in-person history with the designer: meeting him in 1992, owning and
  cruising his boats, the joint Bahamas cruises, and the techniques, figures, and
  words of Matt's they carried back to the list. Primary-goal content on Matt
  Layden himself.
