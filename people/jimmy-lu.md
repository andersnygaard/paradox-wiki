# Jimmy Lu

**Jimmy Lu**, posting as **`jimmylu88`**, built a [Paradox](/paradox.md) in **Taiwan** with no prior boatbuilding experience of his own, simply liking the design. Photos of his work-in-progress, posted to the group from July 2007 onward, showed rapid, professionally finished construction that drew unprompted praise from several established builders and moderators — and Bill Serjeant added him to the official UK-run owners/builders registry the same month he first appeared. The boat launched **18 February 2008**, built in under eight months; her name, **"Auspice,"** does not appear anywhere in the record of the build itself, surfacing only six months later when another builder cited her as a fast-build example for a newcomer. Full build account, the registry listing, and the launch record are on her own page: [Auspice](/boats/auspice.md).
