# Sven Yrvind

Detail pages for [Sven Yrvind](/people/yrvind.md) — the Swedish designer and
ocean voyager who never posted to the Paradox list but is one of its most-cited
outside authorities, the chine runner's foremost external champion, and Matt
Layden's personal friend. The hub page [../yrvind.md](/people/yrvind.md) profiles
him and his role; the pages here hold the detail.

# Concepts

* [Voyages and boats](voyages-and-boats.md) - His ocean-voyaging career (Cape Horn, Tristan da Cunha, the Falklands), his own boats (Bris, Aluminum-Bris, YRVIND.COM, YRVIND ½), and the flex-composite, multi-sail, maximum-safety design philosophy the list contrasts with Matt Layden's.
* [Paradox and the chine runner](paradox-and-the-chine-runner.md) - His firsthand relationship with Matt Layden, the month he sailed Paradox in 1997, his "deflector, not hydrofoil" theory, his "greatest innovation of the century" verdict, and where he ultimately diverges from Matt.
