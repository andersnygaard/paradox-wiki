# Sean Herron

**Sean Herron** (Yahoo account `herrons@…`, numeric id u=136132184) reported his Paradox, **"Defiant,"** under construction and progressing in December 2006, built with slight modifications from Matt Layden's stock design. See [Defiant](/boats/defiant.md) for the boat itself.[1]

Posting from that same account, he had earlier been known on the list only by the signature **"SH"** and the Yahoo handle `liam_lund_macdonald`. Under that handle, in September 2006, he circulated a drawn reinterpretation of Paradox fitted with a full-length skeg carrying an external "barn door" rudder; the proposal drew a detailed objection over how it would shift the boat's balance and sail lead, and was not pursued. See [the full-skeg and barn-door rudder proposal](/components/rudder.md#a-proposed-full-skeg-and-barn-door-rudder-and-why-it-shifts-the-whole-design).[2]
