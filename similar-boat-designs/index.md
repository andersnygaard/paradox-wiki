# Similar boat designs

Boats and designs that are **not the Paradox** but are discussed alongside it — separate microcruisers and small cruisers by other designers, from-scratch derivatives, and cross-design comparisons. This branch is kept apart from the Paradox-specific tree so the core stays focused on the boat itself; content here is about *other* designs, weighed against or inspired by Paradox.

# Concepts

* [KSDuo](ksduo.md) - A from-scratch 17 ft two-person microcruiser expanding a solo "KS2" hull and Paradox alike, strip-built in Western Red Cedar with a raised pop-top cabin, toed-in bilge keels for a shallow dry-out draft, and a carbon-sparred gaff yawl rig.
* [Paradox compared to other small-boat designs](comparisons.md) - How Paradox has been weighed against Jim Michalak's AF3, Robbsboat, and Frolic2, and Phil Bolger's Black Skimmer, Martha Jane, and Cormorant, on stability, seaworthiness, interior room, and build cost.
