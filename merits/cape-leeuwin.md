## Small Fry: rounding Cape Leeuwin, and ultra-shoal cruising of Shark Bay and the Abrolhos

Geoff Davis's Western Australian "Small Fry" rounded **Cape Leeuwin** — where the Indian and Southern Oceans meet — and continued several days offshore without putting in, then cruised the ultra-shoal waters of **Shark Bay**, the **Abrolhos Islands**, and **Jurien Bay**, drying out overnight as a matter of routine rather than incident. Full account at [boats/small-fry.md](/boats/small-fry.md).[1][2]
