# Apalachicola Bay, the Iowa messabout, and a DCA week at Cobnor

Three smaller gathering points round out the Paradox community's meetup record: a solo-scouted Apalachicola Bay, an unconnected Iowa messabout, and a Dinghy Cruising Association week at Cobnor.

## Apalachicola Bay: a proposed meeting ground, scouted solo (2002)

**Apalachicola Bay**, with campgrounds on St. George Island, was an early proposed second gathering ground for Paradox owners — "a wonderful place to camp and sail," with each owner able to inspect and sail the other's boat and swap ideas, and open to other list members and to a more central location if one made better sense. Apalachicola is roughly an 8-hour drive from Memphis.[1]

Rather than wait for a scheduled group date, one owner made a solo scouting trip of the bay in February 2002, launching "[Zoë](/boats/zoe.md)" from Carrabelle Beach on the eastern side of St George Sound (about 30 miles long) and anchoring near St George Island State Park, with an open invitation for anyone in the area to meet up over the weekend — the first record of a Paradox owner actually acting on a proposed group cruise, even though it played out as an individual scouting trip with an open invitation rather than the originally proposed joint outing. Retrieval at low tide there proved uncertain enough to shape later trailer designs (see [trailering — ground clearance for Gulf Coast ramps](/operations/trailering.md#ground-clearance-for-gulf-coast-ramps)).[2]

## The Iowa messabout: a third, unconnected gathering point (proposed 2002)

A general small-boat-builder gathering, not Paradox-specific, was proposed for **Lake Okoboji, northwest Iowa**, for the summer of 2003 — a third, geographically distinct small-boat gathering point alongside Cedar Key and Apalachicola Bay, with no recorded connection to either.[3]

## A DCA week at Cobnor, Chichester Harbour (August 2007)

Two Paradoxes, "Faith" and a second boat, sailed and anchored together for a week at a **Dinghy Cruising Association** meeting at Cobnor, Chichester Harbour, alongside a fleet of DCA dinghies — matching the dinghy fleet's performance on a reach and doing marginally better to windward, and riding out a near-gale at anchor on good holding ground. "Faith"'s own day-by-day log of the same week, including a man-overboard incident, is at [boats/faith.md](/boats/faith.md#the-cobnor-week-a-dinghy-cruising-association-meeting-and-falling-overboard).[4][5]

## A proposed all-Paradox gathering (2010)

A newer builder, working through his own hypothetical future cruising plans — trailering to the Gulf coast and on to the Bahamas, or a longer-shot solo passage to Hawaii once the boat was finished — floated the idea of a large, dedicated Paradox gathering somewhere, hoping for 2010; a reply wondered, half in jest, whether he might already be in Hawaii by then. See [people/rodney-cannon.md — a distant cruising dream, and a proposed Paradox gathering](/people/rodney-cannon.md#a-distant-cruising-dream-and-a-proposed-paradox-gathering) for the fuller exchange.[6][7][8]
