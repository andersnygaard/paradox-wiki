# Apalachicola Bay, the Iowa messabout, and a DCA week at Cobnor

Three smaller gathering points round out the Paradox community's meetup record: a solo-scouted Apalachicola Bay, an unconnected Iowa messabout, and a Dinghy Cruising Association week at Cobnor.

## Apalachicola Bay: a proposed meeting ground, scouted solo (2002)

**Apalachicola Bay**, on Florida's St George Sound, was an early proposed second gathering point; rather than wait for a scheduled group date, one owner made a solo scouting trip there in February 2002 — the first record of a Paradox owner actually acting on a proposed group cruise. Full account at [operations/group-cruise-reports.md](/operations/group-cruise-reports.md#apalachicola-bay-as-a-proposed-meeting-ground).[1]

## The Iowa messabout: a third, unconnected gathering point (proposed 2002)

A general small-boat-builder gathering, not Paradox-specific, was proposed for **Lake Okoboji, northwest Iowa**, for the summer of 2003 — a third, geographically distinct small-boat gathering point alongside Cedar Key and Apalachicola Bay, with no recorded connection to either. Full account at [operations/group-cruise-reports.md](/operations/group-cruise-reports.md#a-third-gathering-point-the-iowa-messabout).[2]

## A DCA week at Cobnor, Chichester Harbour (August 2007)

Two Paradoxes, "Faith" and a second boat, sailed and anchored together for a week at a **Dinghy Cruising Association** meeting at Cobnor, Chichester Harbour, alongside a fleet of DCA dinghies — matching the dinghy fleet's performance on a reach and doing marginally better to windward, and riding out a near-gale at anchor on good holding ground. Full account at [operations/group-cruise-reports.md](/operations/group-cruise-reports.md#a-dca-week-at-cobnor-chichester-harbour-august-2007); "Faith"'s own day-by-day log of the same week, including a man-overboard incident, is at [boats/faith.md](/boats/faith.md#the-cobnor-week-a-dinghy-cruising-association-meeting-and-falling-overboard).[3][4]
