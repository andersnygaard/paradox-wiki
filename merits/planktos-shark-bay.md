## Planktos: a junk-rigged shakedown cruise to Shark Bay

Roselt Croeser's "Planktos" — the archive's only completed, launched, and cruised junk-rig Paradox conversion — made her shakedown cruise from Perth to **Shark Bay**, roughly 800 km up the Western Australian coast, reported to a boatbuilders' association meeting alongside three further passages "contemplated" but not yet attempted: Perth to Shark Bay again, the Abrolhos and back, and Cape Leeuwin and back. Full account at [boats/planktos.md](/boats/planktos.md#shark-bay-and-what-was-contemplated-after).[1]
