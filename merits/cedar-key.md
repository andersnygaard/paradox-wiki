## Cedar Key: the annual small-boat messabout (2003–2019)

The **Cedar Key** small-boat gathering, held the first weekend of May off Florida's Gulf coast, is the community's longest-running meeting point. Two Paradoxes, "Zoë" and "Whisper," attended together as early as 2003, drawing a *Small Craft Advisor* magazine feature that praised both boats; Matt Layden himself attended repeatedly through the 2000s and gave a dinner talk on his design lineage in 2019, with author Ida Little in the audience. Full year-by-year attendance record at [operations/group-cruise-reports.md](/operations/group-cruise-reports.md#a-second-gathering-point-the-cedar-key-small-boat-meet) and [operations/group-cruise-reports.md](/operations/group-cruise-reports.md#the-bolducs-cruising-log-and-buddy-boating-with-matt-layden-microcruisingcom); the magazine coverage is at [operations/group-cruise-reports.md](/operations/group-cruise-reports.md#press-coverage-the-2003-small-craft-advisor-cedar-key-article).[1][2][3]
