# Defiant

**"Defiant"** is [Sean Herron](/people/sean-herron.md)'s [Paradox](/paradox.md), under construction as of December 2006. Sean reported the build progressing well, with **slight modifications** from Matt Layden's stock design, and posted photographs to an external gallery rather than to the list directly.

The photos drew a practical construction tip — mounting the stern lockers' dividers square to the (curved) locker floor rather than vertical, for easier stowage and reach, per [interior — rear-locker divider geometry](/components/interior.md#rear-locker-divider-geometry-and-where-to-stow-heavy-ground-tackle) — and a direct question on whether "Defiant" would sail or run on motor power alone.[2]
