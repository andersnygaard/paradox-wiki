# Defiant

**"Defiant"** is **Sean Herron**'s [Paradox](/paradox.md), under construction and progressing as of December 2006, built with **slight modifications** from Matt Layden's stock design. Sean posted build photographs to an external gallery rather than to the list directly. He posted from a Yahoo account (`herrons@…`, numeric id u=136132184); this page is the canonical home for both the boat and its builder.[1]

Posting from that same account, Herron had earlier been known on the list only by the signature **"SH"** and the Yahoo handle `liam_lund_macdonald`. Under that handle, in September 2006, he circulated a drawn reinterpretation of Paradox fitted with a full-length skeg carrying an external "barn door" rudder; the proposal drew a detailed objection over how it would shift the boat's balance and sail lead, and was not pursued. See [the full-skeg and barn-door rudder proposal](/components/rudder.md#a-proposed-full-skeg-and-barn-door-rudder-and-why-it-shifts-the-whole-design).[2]

# Reception

The build photos drew a practical construction tip — mounting the stern lockers' dividers square to the (curved) locker floor rather than vertical, for easier stowage and reach, per [interior — rear-locker divider geometry](/components/interior.md#rear-locker-divider-geometry-and-where-to-stow-heavy-ground-tackle) — and a direct question on whether "Defiant" would sail or run on motor power alone.[3]
